Embrace digital transformation. Implement SAP

Intelligent Enterprise strategies can go a long way in helping organizations harness the power of automation, drive innovation, and propel growth. Our expert SAP implementation services help you do just that.

Following effective SAP implementation guidelines, we help you plan and streamline your business operations with ease. Through the implementation, you also enjoy end-to-end support from our team who will guide you across the phases of implementation, and help you make the most of the features, inbuilt and add-on.

Our Implementation process is broken down into five phases to ensure the easy set-up of SAP. This includes:

Project Preparation

Analyzing objectives, planning strategies, and defining project metrics.

Business Blueprint

Documenting current business processes, discovering new insights, and redefining project goals and strategies.

Project Realization

Configuring and implementing SAP Business One and SAP Business ByDesign, carrying out test-runs, and revising the setus based on results.

Final Preparation

Training users and administrators, addressing open issues, and upgrading business systems.

Go-live and Support

Completing the SAP Business One implementation and enabling digital transformation.
